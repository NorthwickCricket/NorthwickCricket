About

Here at Northwick Cricket Bat Repairs, our goal isn’t just to help your bats feel fresh but to change the cricket culture in America. With unpredictable weather and drastic seasonal changes, cricket bats are prone to damage in the USA. However, our team realized that due to high pricing for bat repairs, many players rely on just buying a new bat instead of repairing the one they are already attached to. With our affordable and reasonable pricing, we are here to change cricket culture by giving all cricket bats a second life, a second innings.

- Northwick Team

Process

1 2 3 4
1

1. Initial Inquiry

The process starts when you get in touch and share details about your bat and what issues you’re facing. This can include cracks, wear, grip problems, or general performance concerns. The goal at this stage is to get in contact and understand the needs of your service.

2

2. Evaluation & Assessment

Once you share details about your bat, our team will inspect the bat through images or in-person and assess its true condition. This ensures you receive a clear explanation of what work is needed before any repairs begin.

3

3. Repair & Restoration

Your bat is worked on using professional repair techniques including knocking-in, oiling, scuff sheet application, toe guard fitting, edge repair, grip replacement, or binding where needed. Every step is done carefully to improve durability and performance.

4

4. Completion & Pickup

After all work is completed, the bat goes through a final check to ensure everything meets a high standard. It is then cleaned, prepared, and returned to you in match-ready condition, ready for immediate use.

Investment In Your Bat

Every bat is assessed individually to ensure the highest standard of workmanship. Pricing may vary depending on the extent of damage and materials required.
